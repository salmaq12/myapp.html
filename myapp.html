Sun Protection

-Sun protection is essential to prevent premature aging, sunburns, and skin cancer. dark spots, and other signs of aging.

Best practices for sun protection:

  1. Apply sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher every day, even on cloudy days.
  2. Reapply sunscreen every 2 hours, or after swimming or sweating.
  3. Wear protective clothing, hats, and sunglasses for added sun protection.
